## Pacing
- Cuts 1–6 (0–14s): setup, slower is fine.
- Cuts 7–12 (14–27s): keep each clip under ~2–2.5s — this is where it must feel fast and chaotic.
- Keep each overlay on screen ~1–1.5s so it doesn't outstay the cut.

## Overlay style (Clipchamp)
- Font: bold sans-serif (e.g. Montserrat / Poppins Bold).
- High contrast: white or crown-gold `#F5C542` text with a dark drop shadow.
- Position: center for cards 1–3 and 14; lower-third for the gameplay call-outs (7–12).
