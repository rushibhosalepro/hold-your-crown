Hold Your Crown

One crown. Ten knights.
Hold it the longest — and rule the arena.

Real-time brawl · up to 10 players

How to Play

  • Each round is 3 minutes — the most total crown-time wins.
  • Grab the 👑 and hold it to be crowned king.
  • Get knocked out and the crown drops where you fell — anyone can steal it.
  • Move with WASD / arrows or the joystick; attack with the button or space.
  • Grab health & shield drops to survive longer.
  • Live multiplayer, up to 10 — invite friends for a full lobby.
